Tonkatsu

Because who doesn't love fried food.

Photo by Ryan Kwok on Unsplash

Serves: 2 people

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 5 minutes

Total Time: 20 minutes

Fried Japanese

    Ingredients

  • 1 pork cutlet
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1/2 cup of panko
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • Some salt and pepper for seasoning
  • Enough oil for frying

    Tools

  • Stove
  • 1 bowl each for panko, flour, and egg
  • Frying Pan
  • Mallet

Instructions

  1. Smash pork evenly until approx 0.5 cm thick (use ziplock bag or clingwrap for easier cleanup)
  2. Season cutlets with salt and pepper
  3. Put cutlet into bowl of flour and coat evenly, then follow up with egg and panko
  4. Heat up oil in frying pan
  5. Fry cutlet until golden brown
  6. Lay out on paper towls to dry and cool down
  7. Eat with rice, cabbage, curry, or whatever your heart desires
