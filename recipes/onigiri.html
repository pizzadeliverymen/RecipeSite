Onigiri

Rice and Filling, what more could anyone ever need?

Photo by Daniel Téllez Girón on Unsplash

Serves: 2 people

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Japanese Lunch

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • whatever filling you want
  • Seaweed

    Tools

  • Stove/Ricecooker
  • A small bowl and plastic wrap for shaping (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice as you normally would
  2. Prepare fillings
  3. Put plastic wrap in bowl
  4. Dump ~1 large spoonful of rice into bowl
  5. Create small divet in rice to place 2 tsp of fillings into
  6. Use plastic wrap to fold rice over fillings and squeeze to form triangular shape
  7. Repeat with rest of rice/fillings
