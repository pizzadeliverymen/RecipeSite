A short description of Recipe 1.

Vegetarian Quick

Serves: 4 people

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 tbsp of sugar

    Tools

  • Oven
  • Bowl
  • Baking Dish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Pour the mixture into a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
